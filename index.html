Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about timeline, revisions, content, and post-launch support.

How long does it take to build my website?
Most projects go live within 2 to 7 days depending on scope, revisions, and how quickly content is approved.
What do you need from me to get started?
We need a short intake form, your business details, brand preferences, and any images or copy you already have. We guide you through the rest.
Do you offer revisions?
Yes. Every website includes up to 2 revision rounds. Additional revision rounds are $50 each.
Do I need to provide all the content?
Not necessarily. If you already have copy and images, we can use them. If not, we can help structure content and guide you on what to provide.
Will my website be mobile friendly and SEO ready?
Absolutely. Every site is built mobile first, optimized for speed, and follows on page SEO best practices.
Will I be able to update the site after launch?
Yes. We can build your site so you can easily update text, images, and basic content. We also offer support if you want us to handle updates.
Do you provide ongoing support after launch?
Yes. We offer ongoing support plans for updates, technical fixes, and continuous improvements after your site goes live.
